Evaluating the Program

Thank you for taking the time to go through our program! We are interested in your feedback and reactions to the training programs, assessments, and other aspects of the website. Please answer the following questions openly and honestly. Your feedback will help us improve the program.

*Note that assessment sessions were sessions in which you completed questionnaires about your feelings and thoughts. Training sessions were sessions in which you either completed words at the end of sentences OR saw two words on the screen and then a letter that you had to identify by pressing a button on the keyboard.
