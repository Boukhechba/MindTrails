In training, you will read a series of short stories.

For each story:

  • Read the paragraph carefully and really imagine yourself in the situation described.
  • Even if the story describes you reacting in a way that you would not really react, please try to picture yourself responding in the way the story describes.
  • There will be an incomplete word at the end of each paragraph.
  • Press the key or keys on the keyboard that complete the word.
  • When you correctly complete the word you will move on to the next screen and be asked a question about the story.

Press the Space Bar to continue.
