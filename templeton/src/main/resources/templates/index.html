Welcome to Future Thinking

In this study, we are going to focus on the way you think about the future. Specifically, we will focus on the expectations you have about future outcomes.

In this study, you will complete a computer program that will describe different situations and encourage you to think about how those situations will turn out. This computer program is an innovative addition to the field, inspired by similar programs that seem to have positive effects in changing the way we think about the future in order to improve prospection.
