Welcome to MindTrails!

Past studies have shown that changing the way we think can affect our mood, improve our relationships, and make it easier to achieve our goals. For instance, many studies have shown that practice thinking about things in new ways can reduce anxiety symptoms. In the studies below, you can complete a computer program that will describe different situations and encourage you to think about them in particular ways.
